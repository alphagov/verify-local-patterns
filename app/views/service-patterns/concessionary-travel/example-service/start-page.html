You can get a bus pass for free travel when you reach the female State Pension age, whether you’re a man or a woman. Check your state pension age on GOV.UK.

The pass is valid on all local bus services in England at any time on a Saturday, Sunday or bank holiday, and from 9.30am to 11pm on any other day.

If you don't live in {{council.shortName}}, you need to find your local council.

Apply for an older person's bus pass

This service is for new applications. You can also:

  • replace a lost or stolen bus pass.
  • renew an expired bus pass.

About this service

  • This service uses GOV.UK Verify. If you haven’t used GOV.UK Verify before, it takes about 15 minutes to get set up.
  • The bus pass application should take about 5 minutes.
  • You'll upload a digital photo as part of the application.
  • We post your bus pass to you; it should arrive within 5 working days.
  • Once you receive your pass, you'll need it with you whenever you want to use the bus.

If you need assistance using the online service, contact {{ council.name }}.
