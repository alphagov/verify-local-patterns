Other ways to apply

To apply for an older person's bus pass without using GOV.UK Verify you can:

1. Continue without verifying

You can continue to fill out your form online but choose to skip verify, once you have completed your form you will need to bring a form of photo ID and proof of address to the county council in order to collect your pass.

2. Download a paper application

You will need to download, print and complete the application form. You will then need to send the form, along with scanned copies of photo ID and proof of address. Send the form to:

{{council.name}},
1-5 Swishy street,
{{council.shortName}},
AG99 1AX

3. Phone the council on 080038748374834
