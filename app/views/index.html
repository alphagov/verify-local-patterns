Local government service patterns

These guidance pages are for people who work in local government. They outline best practice for delivering digital services.

The service patterns have been designed collaboratively by GDS and the councils involved in the Verify Local pilots. The patterns are based on user research done in Discovery, and were iterated to reflect 4 rounds of testing with real users in Alpha.

The project is now at Beta stage, with councils beginning to implement GDS recommendations.

  • Apply for a resident's parking permit
  • Apply for an older person's concessionary travel pass

To feedback on these patterns, open an issue on Github.
